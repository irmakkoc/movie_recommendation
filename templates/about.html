About MoviOn

Where Emotions Meet Entertainment

Our Mission

At MoviOn, we believe that movies have the power to transform moods, heal emotions, and provide comfort when needed most. Our mission is to connect people with the perfect movies that resonate with their current emotional state.

Using advanced ML technology, we analyze your emotions and recommend movies that can either complement or help transform your mood, creating a personalized viewing experience unlike any other.

What Makes Us Different

Emotion-Driven

Our ML model understands the complex relationship between emotions and cinematic preferences.

Personalized Experience

Every recommendation is tailored to your unique emotional state and preferences.

Vast Movie Library

Access to a carefully curated database of movies across various genres and emotions.

Exclusive Features

Registered users unlock advanced tools like to watch list, watched list, and more.

Meet the Team

This website was developed by Computer Engineering students Irmak Koç & Serkan Açar as our graduation project.

As passionate computer engineers, we set out to merge our technical expertise with the world of entertainment, using emotion analysis to create a unique movie recommendation experience.

We aim to merge technology with entertainment. We intend to provide a worthy website to the users while developing machine learning skills. We believe that technology can be used as a tool for enhancing everyday joy, creativity, and emotional well-being.

We hope MoviOn brings a little more happiness, comfort, and inspiration to your movie nights!

A Letter From Us

In MoviOn we made sure to conduct all the necessary research to recommend you movies at the best service. When identifying your emotions, we use the Emotion Wheel which is recognized by psychologists to guide our approach. We focus on the core emotions located at the center of the wheel for the identification.

Once your emotions are assessed using deep learning and natural language processing, we recommend you movies that will help you get rid of your overwhelming emotions by laughing it off or confronting them. On the other hand, we highly encourage you to maintain your positive emotions, so we recommend movies that will help you with this.

We hope this system brings comfort and joy to you and your emotions.

- MoviOn Team
